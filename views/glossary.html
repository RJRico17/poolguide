    Glossary

    A defintion list of terms used in videos, headings link you the specific videos covering those topics

    Aiming "Ghost Ball"

    A method in where the player lines up an imaginary ghost ball behind the object ball they are attempting to shoot. This technique can also be replace with a cue stick.

    Bridge

    The form your non dominant shooting hand makes in order for a cue stick to smoothly glide through it.

    Draw Shot

    A shot in where bottom spin is imparted in order to make the cue ball come back to the player, also known as backspin

    Follow Shot

    A shot in where top spin is imparted in order to make the cue ball roll forward from the player

    Form

    Specifically the upper body form in where your elbow and head is directly above the cue stick to ensure accurate aim.

    Kick Shot

    A shot where the cue ball hits a rail on the table prior to any contact with an object ball.

    Positional Play

    A strategic plan in order to correctly position the cue ball behind the next ball effeciently.

    Right/Left Spin

    When the cue ball is struct along the X axis. Left or right spin is imparted respective to where the player makes contact.

    Stance

    Specifically the lower body stance in where your dominant foot is over the line of aim while both knees are slightly bent at 45 degrees.

    Stop Shot

    A shot in where stun on the ball is imparted in order for the cue ball to stop dead after contact with an object ball.

    Tangent Line

    A line perpindicular to the point of contact when aiming shots, when played with stun the cue ball will 100% of the time follow this line.

    Top/Bottom Spin

    When the cue ball is struct along the Y axis. Top or bottom spin is imparted respective to where the player makes contact.
