    Welcome to the guide!

    What is this guide?

    The Beginner and Intermediate Billiard Skill Guide is a website and video guide created to teach both beginning and intermediate skills to amateur players. The purpose behind this website was to help beginners and intermediate players alike improve and hone the skills of the game they love.

    How to use this guide

    This website has four main sections. The home, videos, glossary, and contact page.

    Home is where you will find introductory information.

    Videos is where you will find tutorial videos created by RJ.

    Glossary is where you will find specific terms and their descriptions.

    Contact is where you will find RJ's contact information.
